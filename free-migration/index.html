Frequently Asked Questions

How long does migration take?

Typically 2–4 weeks end-to-end, depending on the complexity of your current setup. Simple Mailchimp-to-ActiveCampaign moves with straightforward automations run closer to 2. Complex Klaviyo setups with ecommerce integrations and 30+ flows run closer to 4. I'll give you a specific timeline during the assessment call.

Will I lose email history, contacts, or tags?

No. Complete data preservation is the baseline, not an upsell. Contacts, tags, custom fields, subscribe dates, source attribution — all moved with full fidelity. Engagement history transfers where the source platform exposes it. If a field can't map cleanly, I'll show you the options before we proceed.

What happens to my automations?

Rebuilt, not copied. One of the reasons migrations are worth doing is that your current automations were almost certainly shaped by the platform's limitations. In ActiveCampaign I rebuild them as they should be, with goal-based exits, human-paced wait steps, and a proper tag taxonomy. You get a better program out the other side, not just a transplanted one.

Is it really free?

Yes, for qualified businesses (25K+ contacts, $200+/mo email spend, a real commitment to upgrading). The cost is covered by my ActiveCampaign affiliate relationship — when you sign up for AC through me, I earn a referral fee, and the migration work is my investment in the relationship. No contingent retainer, no strings on the migration itself.

What if I need ongoing help after the migration?

Available as a separate service. Most migration clients want someone to run their program after the move — I offer month-to-month management in the $3–15K range depending on scope. It's a natural continuation, not a requirement. If you just want the migration and then to run it yourself with a trained team, that's a clean handoff and a great outcome too.
